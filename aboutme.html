About Me

Agent Moore - started off as a college freshman that had an innate interest in the technology field. Often called by his family members to "fix their devices", (usually involves changing the batteries but don't tell them that...), decided to work at BestBuy as a computer sales consultant.

After two years of creating lasting connections with clients, Tariq Moore felt it his calling to take up the mantle and become an Agent of Geek Squad.

"Where is he now?", you ask? Will he still answer the call to replace the remote batteries to fix the TV?

Okay I'll stop with the comic book origin story!
I work at Geek Squad as both a Consultation Agent and Advanced Repair Agent with the goal to help make technology easier to understand for the world.

My aspirations are to be a Frontend Developer/Game Developer. I've achieved a great understanding concepts in multiple different programming languages with two years of experience in:
Java, Python, and my favorite The Frontend Trifecta - HTML/CSS/JavaScript.


I'm very passionate about not only frontend development but also music. My room is honestly filled with instruments including piano, ukuleles, saxophones, acoustic guitars, and my latest acquisition, bass guitar.

The world without music is like missing that cup of morning coffee. For me the ability to create something from a blank canvas is a very interesting thing.

I find music production and frontend development to be parallels in that aspect.

They are both forms of art.

Neither of which I can't live without.
